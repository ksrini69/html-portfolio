The Best Movies according to Venkatesh Krishnan

The three best movies of all time

Sholay

This is one of the best bandit movies of all time. Wonderful acting by all concerned. Still so relevant today

Kuch Kuch Hota Hai

A lovely movie by Shah Rukh Khan. Shows a coming of age movie and romance with a real lovely angle to it.

Paheli

Only term to describe this is "Wow". I really liked it due to its theme and the acting.
